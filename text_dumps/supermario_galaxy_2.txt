Note: This text dump is extracted from the North American version of the game,
released May 23, 2010 for the Wii.

What follows is a cleaned-up presentation of all the text in the entire game--
not only dialogue but also menu text, stage lists, etc. At some
points, it was necessary to take minor liberties with the spacing and
formatting of the text's presentation. Text was extracted by
PaperSplash, so this wouldn't have been possible without their help.

As a "clean" text dump, all of the extra bits of control code in the text 
have been removed from this version by me to give it a cleaner presentation. 
For more of my stuff check out the Marioverse Discord Server, the home of 
Mario Lore discussion: https://discord.gg/eq7HVQQ

This is the order in which the text is found in each file, and thus the
order it will be presented in this document.
